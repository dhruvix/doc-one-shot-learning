The datasets have 8 classes: “aadhar_card_full”, “aadhar_card_bottom”, “drivers_license”, “MSRIT_ID_card”, “pan_card”, “passport”, “old_voter_ID_card” and “new_voter_ID_card”
The training dataset has 11 images per class and the test dataset has 5 images per class. 
There are no repeating images in the datasets. 

All this data was collected from Google Images or from people who allowed us to use their identity documents for our project.
